About Visual Estate

Visual Estate is a project developed as part of the COM-480 Data Visualization course at EPFL, within the Master in Data Science program. The topic and dataset were freely chosen, allowing us to explore real estate trends across European cities. Our process involved exploratory data analysis (EDA), insightful storytelling, and the design of compelling visualizations.

You can explore our code and datasets on our GitHub repository.

Organization


Visual Estate is a project developed by a team of passionate individuals who are dedicated to making the real estate process easier for everyone. Our team consists of developers, designers, and real estate experts who work together to provide the best possible experience for our users.
