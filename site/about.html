About Visual Estate

Transforming complex data into clear insights for smarter real estate decisions

Our Mission

Visual Estate is a project developed as part of the COM-480 Data Visualization course at EPFL, within the Master in Data Science program. The topic and dataset were freely chosen, allowing us to explore real estate trends across European cities.

Our process involved exploratory data analysis (EDA), insightful storytelling, and the design of compelling visualizations that make complex market data accessible to everyone.
